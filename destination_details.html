Description

The island's driest months are February and March whereas September sees more rain. To enjoy clear skies, the best time to visit St Martin is the early part of the year. If you're looking for a little bit of everything, April through June is an ideal time to travel.

Day-01

Visit Loterie Farm: A rainforest retreat with a lagoon-style spring water pool, ziplining, hiking trails, and a restaurant

Day-02

Visit Marigot: The capital of the French side, with a main street, open-air market, and Fort Louis ruins

Day-03

Watch the sunset: See the sunset at Tortuga Maho, where you can also watch planes land
